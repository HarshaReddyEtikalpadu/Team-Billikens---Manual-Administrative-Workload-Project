Project Charter

Scope, objectives, and governance for Administrative Workload Analytics

📜

Charter Snapshot

Authorizes the project, aligns stakeholders, and sets the boundaries for delivering the Administrative Workload Analytics Dashboard.

Project Objectives

What we aim to achieve with clear, measurable outcomes

🎯

Visibility

Provide real-time insight into workloads, processing times, and bottlenecks across departments.

⚡

Efficiency

Reduce cycle times and errors through analytics-driven recommendations and staffing alignment.

📊

Decision Support

Equip leadership with evidence for budget, staffing, and automation investments.

Project Scope

✅

In Scope

  • Web dashboard with KPIs, charts, and heatmaps
  • Role-based access for admins and managers
  • Peak-hour analysis and recommendations
  • Synthetic dataset integration (2,600+ records)
🚫

Out of Scope

  • Live system integrations
  • Native mobile apps
  • Automated workflow execution
  • Custom report builder

Project Deliverables

1

Dashboard MVP

Live web app with KPIs, charts, heatmap, and role-based views.

2

Data & Docs

Synthetic dataset, data model, and user/technical documentation.

3

Presentation

Demo, slides, and a concise summary of findings and recommendations.

Team Structure & Responsibilities

Who does what to deliver the charter outcomes

💻

Budget & Resources

💰

Budget

🛠️

Resources

Team laptops, version control, hosting, collaboration tools, and shared documentation.

Risks & Mitigation

1

Scope Creep

Use change control and clear priorities to keep delivery focused.

2

Data Quality

Validate synthetic data and review transformations before release.

3

Adoption

Provide training, intuitive UX, and quick wins to encourage use.
