Problem Context & Stakeholders

Problem Statement

Silverleaf University lacked visibility into administrative workload distribution, processing times, and bottlenecks while processing over 2,600 requests between Jan 2024 and Dec 2025. This led to uneven staffing, long processing times, rework, and a poor service experience.

Key Statistics

  • 2,600+ requests processed (Jan 2024 - Dec 2025)
  • 7 departments: Registrar (30%), Finance (25%), Academic Affairs (20%), IT Support (15%), Student Services (10%), International Office, Others
  • Average processing time: 76 minutes (target: 45–48 minutes)
  • Error rate: ~50% (target <15%)
  • Peak hours: 9–11 AM, Monday–Thursday

Stakeholder Pain Points

  • Department Administrators: Overloaded workflows and unclear priorities.
  • Department Managers: Limited performance visibility to optimize staffing.
  • Senior Management: No consolidated data for budgeting and capacity planning.
  • Students: Indirect impact: delayed services and poor communication.

Impact

Operational delays, increased costs, and lowered student satisfaction. Without analytics, targeted interventions (automation, process redesign, or staffing adjustments) are difficult to justify and implement.
