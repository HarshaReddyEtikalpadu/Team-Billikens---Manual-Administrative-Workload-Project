Research & Insights

Industry Context

Peer research (NACUBO, EDUCAUSE, Deloitte) indicates that higher education institutions can gain 15–22% efficiency improvements through targeted analytics and 60–70% potential for automating repetitive administrative tasks. These findings guided our design choices and prioritized interventions with quick ROI.

Supporting Data

  • Automation potential in back-office processes: 60–70%
  • Document verification process amenable to automation and validation checks
  • Staffing adjustments during peak hours can meaningfully reduce wait times

Academic Citations

  • Deloitte University Insights — Administrative process automation
  • EDUCAUSE — Analytics adoption in higher education
  • NACUBO — Finance and operations best practices

These sources were used to validate assumptions about achievable efficiency gains and to benchmark Silverleaf's potential outcomes against similar institutions.
