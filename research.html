Academic Research Findings

Scholarly evidence linking workload analytics to performance improvement

2022

Workload Transparency & Service Quality

Anderson & Gagliardi | Journal of Higher Education Administration

Key Findings:

  • Workload transparency leads to better staff-to-task fit
  • Reduced service delays, especially during high-volume periods
  • Improved employee satisfaction and reduced burnout
  • Data-driven decisions outperform intuition-based management
Relevance to Silverleaf: Validates our dashboard's focus on transparency and real-time visibility for administrators and managers.
2023

Peak-Hour Analysis & Scheduling Optimization

Li & Chen | International Journal of Educational Management

Key Findings:

  • Identifying peak-hour trends enables proactive scheduling
  • Reduced overtime costs by 18-25% on average
  • Faster turnaround times for cross-department requests
  • Improved student satisfaction scores
Relevance to Silverleaf: Directly supports our peak-hour heatmap feature for optimizing 9-11 AM staffing patterns.
