Proposed Solution Overview

A modern, data-driven platform for administrative workload analytics

💡

What We Built

The Administrative Workload Analytics Dashboard transforms opaque operations into transparent, data-driven workflows. By analyzing more than 2,600 requests across two years, the platform offers real-time insights, pinpoints bottlenecks, and delivers AI-powered recommendations to optimize staffing, routing, and quality.

📊 2,600+ requests analyzed
⏱️ 76 min avg verification time
🧭 7 departments covered
🤖 AI-driven insights surfaced
